Our Heritage

The Legacy of Ceylon

For over 2,500 years, the island formerly known as Ceylon has been the epicenter of the global gemstone trade. Known as Ratnadwipa, the "Island of precious gems" our soil has birthed the world's most legendary Blue Sapphires, Padparadschas and etc.

At Shan Gems, we honor these ancient traditions. Each stone in our collection is ethically sourced directly from underneath of the Ceylon soil, processed by master craftsmen who have passed down the art of the gem cutting through generations.
